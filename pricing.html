Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and features

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes to your subscription will be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all plans. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees or hidden charges. The price you see is the price you pay.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for nonprofit organizations. Please contact our sales team for more information.

What happens when my trial ends?

When your trial ends, you'll be prompted to select a plan to continue using our services. We'll never automatically charge you.
